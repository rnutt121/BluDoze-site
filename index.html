About

BluDoze is a faith-rooted wearable tech brand born from lived experience. Inspired by Psalm 127:2 — "He gives sleep to those He loves" — BluDoze exists to restore peace, rest, and safety for those who need it most. Founded by Robert J. Nutt, BluDoze blends smart technology with emotional care to meet a deeply human need: restful sleep.

Product Preview

Our smart sleepwear uses biometric sensors and Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to monitor heart rate, restfulness, and temperature. The BluDoze mobile app features SafeSleep Mode for unfamiliar environments and soothing audio like white noise, calming voice messages, and prayer. Designed to support healing through tech-enabled comfort.

Who We Help

BluDoze supports individuals in shelters, transitional housing, and reentry programs—those navigating trauma, anxiety, or displacement. Our goal is to restore dignity through rest, offering not just sleep tracking but peace-centered design that comforts body and soul.
